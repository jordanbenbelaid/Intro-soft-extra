Task Overview for Collections/Generics

You are building a small Student Grade Manager that:
Stores students and their grades
Uses generics and collections
Calculates averages
Filters students based on grades

Learning Objectives

By completing this task, students will practice:
Using generic classes
Working with List, Map
Iterating over collections
Writing simple reusable methods
Understanding type safety with generics
